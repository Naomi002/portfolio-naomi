St. Martin Island

St. Martin is the only coral island in Bangladesh. It is famous for its crystal-clear water, sandy beaches, coral reefs, and peaceful environment. It is one of my favorite travel destinations.

Popular Tourist Attractions

  • Chera Dwip
  • Coral Beach
  • St. Martin Jetty
  • Sunset Point

Things to Do

  • Enjoy the sea beach
  • Watch the sunrise and sunset
  • Visit Chera Dwip
  • Taste fresh seafood
  • Take beautiful photographs
