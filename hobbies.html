My Hobbies

Here are some of the activities I love doing in my free time.

Painting

I love expressing myself through colors and brushstrokes. Painting helps me relax, focus, and brings out my creative side. I enjoy working with acrylic and watercolor the most.

Photography

Capturing beautiful moments through my lens is one of my greatest passions. Whether it is nature, portraits, or street photography, I love framing the world from my own perspective.

Travelling

Exploring new places, experiencing different cultures, meeting new people, and trying local foods are things I truly enjoy. Every trip teaches me something new about the world and myself.
